Melbourne International Policy Institute is one of the country’s leading policy think tanks. Based in Melbourne, we conduct high- impact research that combines rigorous fact-driven material with cutting-edge communication strategies.

When we talk about ‘research that matters’ we are talking about making real, tangible changes that would not have happened if we were not there to make them happen.Come join the Melbourne International Policy Institute team!

 

 

CAREERS

--Director of Strategic Communications  

MIPI is seeking an experienced leader in communications and public affairs with a strong track record of delivering key outcomes and developing effective messaging and communication strategies, preferably with experience in foreign, defense, or security policy. 

Reporting directly to the Executive Director, this is a key leadership role with influence across the organization. 

The Director of Strategic Communications will work across MIPI to develop and shape the organization’s communications strategy. This will involve overseeing the MIPI Strategist, the MIPI Podcast, social media, promotion of MIPI products, speech writing, and media engagement. The Director of Strategic Communications will work with all forms of media, including press releases and digital campaigns, print materials, social media, audio, and video. 

As a team, you will develop MIPI’s communications strategy and work across MIPI to deliver our strategic priorities, including influencing key policy and decision makers, reaching broad and diverse audiences, and attracting new partners and supporters.  

The closing date for applications is Friday, 7 Oct 2022 – an early application is advised as we reserve the right to close the vacancy early if suitable applications are received.
